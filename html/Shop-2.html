MyWiFi Xtreme (valued at $9.95 per month) is included with all internet plans. Optional additional mesh units at $2.95/mo may be required for homes of a certain square footage. The wireless connection setup of up to four devices is included in the installation of MyWiFi Xtreme. Devices must be activated with the proper credentials prior to your install appointment. Home Telecom cannot install or set up 3rd party applications on your personal devices. E-bill with Auto-bank draft opt out will increase your monthly bill by $5.00/mo. An activation fee applies for standard installation. Standard installation includes a professionally trained technician and a complete whole home Wi-Fi certification with heat map. Additional installation fees may be required and will be discussed at time of install. Certain restrictions may apply. Not available in all areas. Price is a limited time offer and may be discontinued at any time. Call for details.

Home Telecom is not responsible for troubleshooting or supporting any streaming services presented through MyBundle TV. All support inquiries should be sent to the streaming service provider you subscribe to directly.

All packages are subject to federal, state, local, regulatory fees and taxes which are not included. Users of HomeStream TV service are required to supply their own compatible internet connected devices on which they may download the HomeStream TV app. Not available on all streaming devices. Quality of stream may vary depending upon a variety of factors including the environment, number of devices connected to your internet, age of device, and type of device used for streaming. Recordings for Cloud DVR will be deleted after 1 year. Internet is required for all video service packages. Pricing does not include internet package pricing. Internet speed of 6 Mbps per stream is required to receive HomeStream TV service. In the event you experience an interruption to your internet connection, HomeStream TV will be affected. Restart/Replay is enabled/disabled on an individual show and/or network basis dependent on allowance in content agreements. HomeStream TV products details and channel lineups subject to change. Prices are subject to change. Additional restrictions may apply.
